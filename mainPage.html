Summer fruit basket

This summer fruit basket is a tasty and colorful experience. It contains soft and sweet peaches, refreshing cherries and young apples and herbs. The variety of flavors come together to create a perfect taste symphony, combined with a high level of vitamins, minerals and antioxidants. Everything for a fresh and vital body!

Tropical fruit basket

During a hot day, there's nothing like stopping and savoring a tropical fruit basket. With each mouth opened, aromas of sweet pineapple, soft banana, indulgent apple and Japanese mango come out. The tropical flavors are a vacation experience and your wings are ready to fly!

Colorful fruit basket

This colorful fruit basket is not only an excellent source of vitamins and minerals, but also a strong connection to a variety of flavors. Out of dozens of fruits in the colors of the rainbow, each one provides its unique properties for the benefit of health and maintained taste.

Winter fruit basket

With the cold in the air, this basket qualifies as a great source of vitamins and minerals. Each pomegranate apple and pear will upgrade the edge of the winter cold. Fruits with indulgent flavors will add a new level of warmth and connection to the pleasure of winter flavors.

Amazing fruit basket

With every moment, it is the basket that brings with it privacy and creativity. Crunchy apples, colorful cherries and green bananas create a symphony of flavors and appearance. Suitable for any party or event, it will bring a new chapter of joy and fine flavors to your daily life.

Types of fruit

Peach

Sweet, soft and delicious taste, provides vitamins and dietary fiber.

Apple

From a sweet taste, provides wonderful vitamins and dietary fiber.

Banana

A soft, tasty fruit that provides energy and vitamins without effort.

Pomegranate

A penetrating taste, rich in antioxidants, the fruit of the holiday of the little ones.

Carambola

A unique fruit, sharp and refreshing taste, rich in vitamins.

Watermelon

From coolness, sweet in taste, provides moisture and vitamins.

Persimmon

Small, from an envelope of flavors, provides vitamins and perfect sweetness.

Pineapple

Sea fruit, with a sweet-sour taste, delightful and fresh.

Plum

Soft and sweet fruit, provides fiber and vitamins required for recovery.
